Game Tutorial

Welcome to Puzzle Master!

Puzzle Master is a comprehensive puzzle game featuring four different game modes, each with multiple difficulty levels. Master all modes to become the ultimate Puzzle Master!

Game Features

  • Achievements: Unlock 6 different achievements
  • Progress Tracking: Level up in each puzzle mode
  • Scoring System: Earn points and track your best times
  • Sound Effects: Immersive audio feedback
  • Mobile Support: Play on any device

How to Play

  1. Choose a puzzle mode from the main menu
  2. Select your preferred difficulty level
  3. Complete the puzzle as quickly as possible
  4. Earn points and unlock achievements
  5. Check your progress in the Dashboard

Sliding Puzzle

Arrange numbered tiles in order by sliding them into the empty space. Only tiles adjacent to the empty space can be moved.

How to Play

  1. Click on any tile adjacent to the empty space
  2. The tile will slide into the empty position
  3. Continue until all tiles are in numerical order
  4. The empty space should be in the bottom-right corner

Difficulty Levels

  • 3x3 (Easy): 8 tiles to arrange
  • 4x4 (Medium): 15 tiles to arrange
  • 5x5 (Hard): 24 tiles to arrange

Tips

  • Start by placing the first row, then the second row
  • Work systematically from top to bottom
  • Use the shuffle button if you get stuck

Jigsaw Puzzle

Drag and drop puzzle pieces to complete the image. Place all pieces in the correct positions to solve the puzzle.

How to Play

  1. Click and drag any puzzle piece
  2. Drop it in the correct position
  3. Pieces will snap into place when positioned correctly
  4. Complete all pieces to finish the puzzle

Difficulty Levels

  • 12 pieces (Easy): Large, easy-to-place pieces
  • 24 pieces (Medium): Medium-sized pieces
  • 48 pieces (Hard): Small, challenging pieces

Tips

  • Start with corner pieces and edge pieces
  • Look for distinctive colors or patterns
  • Group similar pieces together

Memory Match

Find matching pairs of cards by flipping them over. Remember the positions of symbols to make successful matches.

How to Play

  1. Click on any card to flip it over
  2. Click on a second card to see if it matches
  3. If they match, both cards stay flipped
  4. If they don't match, both cards flip back
  5. Find all matching pairs to win

Difficulty Levels

  • 8 pairs (Easy): 16 cards total
  • 12 pairs (Medium): 24 cards total
  • 18 pairs (Hard): 36 cards total

Tips

  • Pay attention to card positions
  • Try to remember where you've seen each symbol
  • Take your time - there's no time limit

Word Search

Find hidden words in the letter grid. Words can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal in any direction.

How to Play

  1. Click on letters to select them
  2. Select letters in sequence to form words
  3. Words must be at least 3 letters long
  4. Found words will be highlighted
  5. Find all hidden words to complete the puzzle

Difficulty Levels

  • 8x8 (Easy): Smaller grid with fewer words
  • 12x12 (Medium): Medium grid with more words
  • 16x16 (Hard): Large grid with many words

Tips

  • Look for common letter patterns
  • Check all directions: horizontal, vertical, diagonal
  • Start with shorter words first

General Tips

  • Take your time: There's no rush - focus on accuracy
  • Use hints: Click the hint button if you get stuck
  • Practice regularly: The more you play, the better you get
  • Try different difficulties: Start easy and work your way up

Scoring Tips

  • Speed bonus: Complete puzzles quickly for extra points
  • Fewer moves: Efficient solutions earn more points
  • No hints: Avoid using hints for maximum score
  • Streaks: Win multiple games in a row for bonus points

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Escape - Return to main menu
  • R - Shuffle current puzzle
  • H - Show hint
  • Space - Pause/Resume game

Achievements

Unlock achievements by completing specific challenges:

  • First Steps: Complete your first puzzle
  • Speed Demon: Complete a puzzle in under 30 seconds
  • Perfectionist: Complete 10 puzzles without hints
  • Streak Master: Win 5 games in a row
  • Puzzle Master: Complete all puzzle modes
  • High Scorer: Reach 10,000 total points
